## EP Strategy

### Release Schedule
- **Week 1-2:** Track 1 & 2 (establish foundation)
- **Week 3-4:** Track 3 & 4 (deepen understanding)
- **Week 5-6:** Track 5 & 6 (challenge assumptions)
- **Week 7:** Track 7 (finale + call to action)

### Visual Strategy
- Each track gets:
  - Lyric video with archival documents
  - 15-second TikTok/Reels hook
  - Educational breakdown post
  - Source citations in description

### Educational Materials
- Companion study guide
- Source bibliography for each track
- Discussion questions
- Recommended reading list

### Target Audience
- Truth-seekers, not blind believers
- Students and educators
- History enthusiasts
- Those questioning official narratives
- People researching their ancestry

### Platform Strategy
- **SoundOn/TikTok**: Primary distribution
- **YouTube**: Long-form with b-roll
- **Spotify**: Album format with liner notes
- **Instagram**: Carousel posts with key facts
- **Twitter/X**: Thread breakdowns

## Why This Works

1. **Modular Learning**: One concept per track
2. **Progressive Depth**: Each track builds on previous
3. **Evidence-Based**: No conspiracy, just documented history
4. **Platform-Safe**: Educational content, not inflammatory
5. **Repeatable**: Hooks designed for retention
6. **Shareable**: Each track stands alone but connects to whole

## Success Metrics

- **Educational Impact**: Comments showing learning
- **Source Requests**: People asking for references
- **Discussion Quality**: Nuanced conversation, not arguments
- **Cross-Platform Reach**: Content shared across platforms
- **Long-term Value**: Tracks remain relevant over time
