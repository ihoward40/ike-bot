## Songs

### 1. Names & Crowns (R&B/Neo-Soul)
**Purpose:** Teach that the fight over labels (Moors, Africans, etc.) is a distraction  
**Key Message:** Understanding continuity is stronger than arguing labels  
**Tempo:** 72 BPM  
**Mood:** Calm authority, reflective, confident

**Core Lyric:**
> "It ain't Moors vs us, nah, that's the trick  
> Same roots, different ships"

### 2. Before the Boats (Drill Remix)
**Purpose:** Educational drill exposing historical reclassification  
**Key Message:** Documented presence predates official narratives  
**Tempo:** 140 BPM  
**Mood:** Aggressive facts, no emotion

**Core Lyric:**
> "Before the boats, documented presence  
> Dark skin natives in explorer sentences"

### 3. Paper Genocide (Track 2)
**Purpose:** Explain administrative erasure of identity  
**Key Message:** Census and legal tools erased Indigenous recognition  
**Historical Focus:** Virginia Racial Integrity Act (1924)

**Core Lyric:**
> "Census ain't neutral, it's policy tech  
> One checkbox decide who own land next"

## Historical Research

### Black American Origins: Indigenous Muurs vs. Mainstream History

Comprehensive research document examining competing narratives about Black American origins.

**Methodology:**
- Comparative analysis of mainstream vs. suppressed accounts
- 17 primary and secondary sources
- Transparent about evidence vs. interpretation
- No predetermined conclusions

**Key Topics Covered:**
- Early European explorer accounts (Verrazzano, Balboa, Columbus)
- "Paper genocide" through census reclassification
- Virginia Racial Integrity Act (1924) and legal erasure
- Institutional motives for narrative control
- Research strategies for verification

**Approach:** Documentation over ideology, inquiry over certainty

## Philosophy

This project operates on core principles:

1. **Documentation over opinion** - Primary sources emphasized
2. **Inquiry over conclusion** - No forced beliefs required
3. **Process over message** - Teach HOW to evaluate, not WHAT to believe
4. **Evidence-based** - Claims separated from interpretation
5. **Platform-safe** - Educational content, not inflammatory
6. **Durable** - Content designed to remain relevant over time
