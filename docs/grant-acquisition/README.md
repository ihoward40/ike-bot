# Grant Acquisition Division - Foundation Build

**Strategic Overview**: Centralized grant pipeline management system for securing funding to support IKE Solutions operations, legal defense, and community impact initiatives.

## Quick Navigation

1. [01-NOTION-PIPELINE-SETUP.md](01-NOTION-PIPELINE-SETUP.md) - Grant pipeline database schema
2. [02-MASTER-ORG-PROFILE.md](02-MASTER-ORG-PROFILE.md) - Organization narrative and boilerplate
3. [03-OUTREACH-TEMPLATES.md](03-OUTREACH-TEMPLATES.md) - LOI and cold outreach email templates
4. [04-FOCUS-AREAS.md](04-FOCUS-AREAS.md) - Priority funding targets and strategy

## System Purpose

**Problem Solved**: Eliminate redundant application prep, missed deadlines, and lost opportunities by creating a centralized command center for grant discovery, tracking, and execution.

**Key Benefits**:
- ✅ Never miss a deadline with date-based tracking
- ✅ Reuse organization narrative across applications
- ✅ Prioritize high-impact opportunities
- ✅ Track required documents and submission status
- ✅ Maintain funder relationships and contact history

## Quick Start (20 Minutes)

1. **Create Notion Database** (5 min)
   - Follow [01-NOTION-PIPELINE-SETUP.md](01-NOTION-PIPELINE-SETUP.md)
   - Import schema with 15+ properties
   - Configure views for active, pending, and won opportunities

2. **Import Org Profile** (5 min)
   - Review [02-MASTER-ORG-PROFILE.md](02-MASTER-ORG-PROFILE.md)
   - Customize narrative for your organization
   - Save as template for quick copy-paste

3. **Prepare Outreach Email** (5 min)
   - Use templates from [03-OUTREACH-TEMPLATES.md](03-OUTREACH-TEMPLATES.md)
   - Personalize for your first target funder
   - Send and track in pipeline

4. **Define Priorities** (5 min)
   - Review focus areas in [04-FOCUS-AREAS.md](04-FOCUS-AREAS.md)
   - Tag your top 3 funding priorities
   - Begin researching matching opportunities

## Pipeline Workflow

```
Discovery → Research → Application → Submission → Follow-up → Award/Decline
    ↓           ↓            ↓             ↓              ↓            ↓
  Notion    Notion      Notion        Notion        Notion       Notion
   Log      Update      Draft         Track         Log          Archive
```
