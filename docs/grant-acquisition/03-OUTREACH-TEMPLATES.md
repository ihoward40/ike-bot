## Timing Guidelines

**Initial Outreach**: 
- 60-90 days before application deadline (for foundations with cycles)
- Anytime for foundations with rolling deadlines

**Follow-Up #1**:
- 7-10 business days after initial email (if no response)

**Follow-Up #2** (optional):
- 14 days after Follow-Up #1 (only if deadline is approaching)

**Post-Submission Check-In**:
- 2-3 weeks after submission (if no timeline given)
- Only if you haven't heard anything and deadline for decisions has passed

**Thank You Notes**:
- Within 24 hours of meeting/call
- Within 24 hours of award notification
