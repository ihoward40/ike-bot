Frequently Asked Questions

How fast can you turn around my documents?

Standard turnaround is 2 business days. We offer next-business-day rush service for an additional fee. Complex packets (3+ documents) typically take 3–5 business days. We respond to all intakes within 24 hours.

Are you a law firm? Do you provide legal advice?

No. IKE SOLUTIONS is not a law firm. We provide document preparation, case strategy consultation, and administrative support. We do not provide legal advice or represent clients in court. We recommend you consult an attorney if you need legal advice.

What if I'm not sure what service I need?

Start with a Case Review & Strategy Audit ($49–$97). We'll analyze your situation and recommend the right next steps, whether that's document drafting, a packet, or additional guidance.

Can you help with my specific situation?

We specialize in debt disputes, collection defense, affidavits, declarations, motions, and filing preparation for self-represented litigants. Complete your intake form and we'll let you know if we can help.

What if I need to revise my documents?

We include minor revisions and clarifications as part of our service. For substantial rewrites, we'll discuss additional fees upfront.

How do I know my information is secure?

We treat all client information as confidential. Your intake form and documents are encrypted and stored securely. We do not share your information with third parties.

What formats will my documents be in?

Documents are delivered as Word (.docx) or PDF files that you can easily edit, print, or file. All documents are formatted for court submission and filing.

Do you guarantee results?

No. We cannot guarantee specific outcomes or results. Our job is to prepare you with clear, well-organized documents and strategic guidance. The outcome of your case depends on many factors beyond our control, including the judge, evidence, and your opponent's actions.

Can you file documents on my behalf?

No. We prepare documents for YOU to file. Filing is your responsibility. However, we'll provide clear instructions on how and where to file.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept credit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers. Once you complete your intake, we'll provide a quote and payment options.
